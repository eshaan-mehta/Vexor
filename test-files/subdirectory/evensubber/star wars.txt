Star Wars Original Trilogy Summary
----------------------------------

1. Episode IV – A New Hope (1977)
----------------------------------
The story begins with Princess Leia being captured by the Empire while trying to deliver plans for the Death Star, a powerful space station. These plans end up with Luke Skywalker, a young farm boy on Tatooine. After meeting Obi-Wan Kenobi, an old Jedi Knight, Luke learns of his heritage and the Force. With the help of Han Solo, Chewbacca, Leia, and the droids R2-D2 and C-3PO, Luke joins the Rebel Alliance. The film climaxes with Luke destroying the Death Star using the Force.

2. Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back (1980)
----------------------------------------------
The Rebels are attacked by the Empire on the ice planet Hoth. Luke travels to the planet Dagobah to train with Jedi Master Yoda. Meanwhile, Han, Leia, and their companions are pursued by Darth Vader. The film ends in Cloud City where Vader captures Han and reveals to Luke that he is his father. Han is frozen in carbonite and taken by bounty hunter Boba Fett. The movie ends on a somber note, with the heroes regrouping and preparing to rescue Han.

3. Episode VI – Return of the Jedi (1983)
------------------------------------------
The film opens with a daring mission to rescue Han from Jabba the Hutt. Afterward, the Rebels plan an attack on a second Death Star. Luke confronts Vader and the Emperor, trying to redeem his father. In the final battle, Vader turns against the Emperor to save Luke but dies in the process. The Death Star is destroyed, and the galaxy celebrates the fall of the Empire. Luke cremates Vader’s body and sees the spirits of Obi-Wan, Yoda, and Anakin Skywalker at peace.

----------------------------------
Themes: Heroism, redemption, resistance against tyranny, the struggle between good and evil.
